📘 About the Group

This student-led initiative is designed for dedicated, full-time learners in IGNOU BCA (New) Semester 3. Our mission is to foster collaborative learning, logic building, and hands-on programming in C, C++, Python, DBMS, and OS. Members benefit from peer support, resource sharing, and interactive study sessions to maximize their academic and practical skills.

👥 Admin Team Roles

  • Lead Admin: Manages group structure, strategy, and discipline.
  • Content Admin: Shares weekly study material and resources.
  • Engagement Admin: Welcomes members and boosts group participation.

Admins ensure a friendly, inclusive, and focused environment for all members.

📌 Group Policies

  • Respect everyone and keep the space distraction-free
  • Only study-related discussions allowed
  • No AI-generated answers for assignments
  • Stay active and contribute to learning

Policies ensure a fair, focused, and helpful environment for all.

By participating, you’ll build:

  • Solid technical foundation (C, C++, Python, DBMS, OS)
  • Leadership, collaboration, and communication skills
  • Discipline to study consistently with a team
  • Experience managing and engaging in a learning community
